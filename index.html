About A One Clothing

A One Clothing is a local outdoor lifestyle store in Kingston, Ontario. We carry clothing, footwear, gear and accessories to keep you comfortable in every season — whether you’re commuting downtown, hiking the trails, or traveling.

We are known for friendly service and quality brands. Our goal is simple: help you find the right fit and the right gear, every time.

Our motto: A1 Brands. A1 Price. A1 Service.

Serving Kingston since 1938.
